🌸 Complete Makeup Guide 🌸

Learn how to use each makeup product correctly for a glowing, flawless look

Primer

Apply a thin layer before foundation to smooth skin and help makeup last longer.

Foundation

Even out your skin tone by choosing the right shade. Apply with brush, sponge, or fingers.

Concealer

Use to cover dark circles, blemishes, or imperfections. Blend gently into skin.

Powder

Set your foundation with powder to reduce shine and extend makeup wear.

Blush

Add color to your cheeks. Smile and lightly apply to the apples of your cheeks.

Eyeshadow

Enhance your eyes with colors that complement your look. Blend well.

Eyeliner

Define your eyes. Apply close to the lash line for a natural or dramatic effect.

Mascara

Coat lashes from root to tip for volume and length. Apply one or two layers.

Lipstick

Apply after lip balm. Use a lip liner for perfect shape and longer wear.

Bronzer

Add warmth and dimension to your face. Apply to cheekbones, temples, and jawline.

Setting Spray

Hold the spray 20 cm/8 inches away and mist evenly over your face to set makeup.
